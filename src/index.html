Trending Recipes

Card Rwandan brochettes

Rwandan brochettes are popular street food in Rwanda. Skewered and grilled, they feature tender and marinated meat, such as beef, goat, or chicken. Served with grilled vegetables and condiments like akabanga sauce, they offer a flavorful and satisfying dining experience, showcasing Rwanda's love for grilled meats.

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish. It features tender chicken cooked in a tomato-based sauce enriched with butter, cream, and aromatic spices. The creamy and flavorful curry is typically served with naan bread or rice, and it has gained global recognition for its rich taste and indulgent texture.

Ukrainian borscht

Ukrainian borscht is a traditional beet-based soup from Ukraine. It's a vibrant and hearty dish, typically made with beets, vegetables, and meat. The soup has a sweet and tangy flavor, often served hot with sour cream and fresh herbs. It's a beloved symbol of Ukrainian cuisine, enjoyed for its rich flavors and comforting nature.
