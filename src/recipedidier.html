INGREDIENTS

2 courgettes

Poivre et sel

1 oignon rouge

1 poivron rouge

1 poivron orange

450 g (1 lb) de dinde hachée

15 ml (1 c. à soupe) de paprika fumé

2,5 ml (1/2 c. à thé) de poudre d'ail

2,5 ml (1/2 c. à thé) de cumin moulu

125 ml (1/2 tasse) de sauce BBQ du commerce, divisée

PREPARATION

Finely chop half the red onion. Place in a large bowl.

Cut the rest of the onion and the peppers into cubes. Cut the zucchini into rounds.

In the bowl containing the chopped onion, add the turkey, 60 ml (1/4 cup) of BBQ sauce and the spices. Mix well. Pepper generously and add a pinch of salt.

With your hands, form 12 meatballs about 2.5 cm (1 inch) in diameter.

Preheat barbecue on high to about 290°C (550°F).

Thread meatballs onto skewers, alternating with vegetables.

Reduce the power of the barbecue to medium heat to reach about 200°C (400°F).

Place the skewers on the barbecue grill, close the lid and cook for 12 to 15 minutes. During cooking, turn the skewers twice and brush with the remaining BBQ sauce (60 ml / 1/4 cup) using a pastry brush.

Serve with grilled bells, if desired.
